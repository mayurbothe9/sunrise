About us

In April 2008, the Goddess Saraswati Educational Society established one of the top schools in Delhi NCR(Sonipat) i.e. Sunrise International School. With a vision to impart quality education to the children for their overall development and by giving due stress on Indian Values which are respected throughout the world, the young mind will be prepared to act as the messenger for world peace. They will help to form a society free of prejudices such as religion, caste, creed, and colour. They will work for the benefit of the human race and their only religion will be humanity. They will follow the motto of "Service before Self". The students of this boarding school are prepared for their overall development. So that they will face the challenges in this competitive world later on at each step boldly and courageously. The most modern technologies are used in the school while imparting educational instructions to the students so that a permanent impact may be left on their minds. Our mission is not only to cater to its students their academic brilliance needs but also to prepare them for their later life to work for the betterment of mankind so that a society may be formed which is free from corruption, ill-feelings, ill-will, and mistrust

Aim

A school has four walls and a future inside. Each school strongly believes in the utmost development of a child that helps them nurture, shape their skills which in turn helps them to stand out in a huge crowd with their own unique abilities and talent. The Shri Ram Universal School comprehends to imbibe the exact same values in their students. They create an atmosphere for students that is joyous and vibrant engages them more in their study material with the zeal to do better every time.
